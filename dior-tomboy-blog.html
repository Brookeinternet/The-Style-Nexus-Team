The Dior Tomboy Aesthetic

The ensemble Aura presents is a masterclass in understated luxury. We see tailored trousers with a relaxed fit, perhaps in a refined wool or a crisp cotton blend, paired with a subtly oversized blazer that exudes an air of nonchalant elegance. The clean lines and muted color palette, characteristic of Dior's contemporary collections, contribute to the outfit's sophisticated yet accessible appeal.

What makes this look truly "tomboyish" is the deliberate choice of relaxed silhouettes and minimal adornment, allowing the quality of the fabrics and the precision of the tailoring to speak for themselves. Aura's styling emphasizes comfort without sacrificing an ounce of chic, making it a perfect inspiration for those who appreciate a blend of masculine-inspired cuts with feminine grace. The subtle details, like a perfectly rolled cuff or the way the fabric drapes, elevate the entire look.
